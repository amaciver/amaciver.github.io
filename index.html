Who I Am

What I really love is solving problems and finding patterns. For me that has taken many, many forms, including studying the way music and shapes can be analyzed mathematically and generalized to produce compelling computer-generated art. I have a keen interest in machine learning as well as human learning, and I am intrigued by the ways in which artificial intelligence, neuroscience, and pedagogy can be fused to improve the effectiveness and accessibility of education.
