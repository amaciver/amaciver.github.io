CouchSmurfing is a full-stack web application inspired by couchsurfing where users can books stays with their favorite cartoon characters from the 1980's. Built on Ruby on Rails with a React/Redux frontend, users accounts are validated for authenticity and their requests and reviews persist in their profile. With a fine attention to user expereience and esthetics, this project highlights the broad scope of technologies, APIs, and frameworks I use.
